The strategy for this policy is based on two key principles:

1. **Prioritize immediate captures**: Always take advantage of moves that complete one or more boxes, as this grants an additional turn and denies the opponent the same opportunity.
2. **Defensive play**: If no captures are possible, choose a move that minimizes opportunities for the opponent to capture boxes on their next turn by avoiding moves that would create boxes with exactly three sides filled.

By combining these approaches, the AI maximizes offensive opportunities while strategically limiting the opponent's potential gains.
