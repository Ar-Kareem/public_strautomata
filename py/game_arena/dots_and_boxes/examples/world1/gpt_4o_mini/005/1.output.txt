2. **Immediate Captures**: If a move can complete a box, it should be made. If multiple boxes can be completed, choose the move that maximizes the number of boxes captured.

3. **Move Selection**: When there is no immediate capture available, select the move that maximizes your advantages while minimizing the opponent's potential. Prefer moves that do not lead to three sides of a box being filled.
