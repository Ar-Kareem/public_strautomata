I'll design a smart policy for Dots and Boxes that focuses on both immediate gains and long-term strategy. Here's my approach:

1. **Immediate Win Check**: First, look for moves that complete a box (immediate score)
2. **Forced Moves**: If opponent has a potential double-cross, block it
3. **Chain Reactions**: Look for moves that create multiple scoring opportunities
4. **Control Strategy**: Aim to control the center and create balanced positions
5. **Safe Moves**: If no immediate opportunities, make moves that don't give opponent easy points

The policy will prioritize:
- Completing boxes when possible (immediate score)
- Blocking opponent's potential double-crosses
- Creating chain reactions where possible
- Making moves that don't create vulnerable positions
