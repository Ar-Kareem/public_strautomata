# Strategy for Dots and Boxes Policy

This policy implements a smart strategy for Dots and Boxes on a 4x4 grid with the following key components:

1. **Immediate Win Check**: First checks if there are any moves that would complete a box (scoring move). These are prioritized since they give an extra turn.

2. **Double Cross Prevention**: Avoids creating situations where the opponent can score multiple boxes in one move (double crosses).

3. **Chain Reaction Detection**: Looks for moves that could trigger chain reactions (multiple consecutive scoring moves).

4. **Control of Center**: Prioritizes moves in the center of the board to gain more control over potential scoring opportunities.

5. **Edge Control**: When no immediate scoring moves are available, focuses on controlling edges that limit opponent options.

6. **Random Fallback**: If no strategic moves are found, makes a random legal move to ensure the policy always returns a valid move.

The policy balances between aggressive scoring and defensive play while maintaining board control. It uses numpy for efficient array operations to stay within the 1-second time constraint.
