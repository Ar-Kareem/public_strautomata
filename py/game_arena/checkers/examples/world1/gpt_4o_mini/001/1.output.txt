The strategy for this policy is centered around several key principles of checkers:

1. **Capture Priority**: Always prioritize capturing the opponent's pieces when possible, as this will help reduce their strength on the board.

2. **Piece Safety**: Ensure my pieces are not left vulnerable to be captured by the opponent. This includes keeping them on the back row to prevent being jumped over easily.

3. **Promotion Considerations**: Aim to promote pieces to kings by advancing towards the opponent's back row but only when it's safe to do so.

4. **Control of the Board**: Maintain control over the center of the board as it allows for more movement options and better opportunities to capture opponents.

5. **Minimax Strategy with Heuristics**: Implement a simple minimax-like strategy with heuristics to evaluate the best moves based on piece count and position strength.

The implementation will evaluate all possible moves and prioritize those consisting of captures, while checking for potential threats against my pieces and leaving options for future moves.

        moves = []
        row, col = piece
        directions = []

        # Determine moves based on the color since they move in different directions
        if color == 'b':
            directions = [(1, -1), (1, 1)]  # Moving down the board

            directions = [(-1, -1), (-1, 1)]  # Moving up the board
