3. **Destroy mode**: Once we know the orientation (have 2+ hits in a line), continue firing along that line in both directions.

4. **Probability-based targeting**: In hunt mode, prioritize cells where larger ships are more likely to fit based on remaining unknown cells.

- Use checkerboard pattern (parity) in hunt mode - ships of length 2+ will always occupy at least one cell of each parity
- When we get a hit, immediately check all 4 adjacent cells
- When we have 2+ hits in a row, follow the line
