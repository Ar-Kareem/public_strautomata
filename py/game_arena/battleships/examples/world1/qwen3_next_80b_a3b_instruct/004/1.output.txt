2. **Targeting Phase**: Once a hit is found, switch to a targeted mode. Use a flood-fill like BFS to explore adjacent cells (up, down, left, right) to determine ship orientation and length. This systematically hunts along the suspected ship axis.

3. **Priority Ordering**: When multiple targets are available, prioritize cells that are:

5. **Fallback**: If no hits exist and the checkerboard is exhausted (unlikely before ~50 shots), fall back to uniform random sampling among unknown cells.
