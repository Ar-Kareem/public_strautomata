### Approach
The approach involves simulating each possible move and evaluating the resulting board state to determine the best move. The key steps are:
1. **Generate Distribution Order**: Based on the chosen house, generate the order in which seeds will be distributed.
2. **Simulate the Move**: Simulate the distribution of seeds according to the generated order, including capturing seeds from the opponent if possible.
3. **Evaluate the Move**: After simulating the move, evaluate the new state of the board to determine the best move. The evaluation considers the number of seeds in your store, the opponent's store, and whether the move results in an extra turn.

        order.extend(range(start, 6))

### Explanation
1. **Generate Distribution Order**: The `generate_order` function creates a list of positions (house numbers) that seeds will be distributed to, starting from the next house after the chosen one, through the player's store, the opponent's houses, and then back to the player's houses.
2. **Simulate Move**: The `simulate_move` function generates a new state of the board after distributing seeds according to the generated order. It also checks for captures if the last seed lands in an empty house of the player and the opponent's corresponding house has seeds.
