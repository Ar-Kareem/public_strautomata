1. **Checkmate Detection**: Always prioritize moves that lead to checkmate or avoid being checkmated.

2. **Material Evaluation**: Prioritize capturing higher-value pieces over lower-value ones, with special consideration for pawn promotion.

3. **King Safety**: Avoid moves that expose the king to attacks or reduce its safety.

4. **Piece Mobility**: Prefer moves that increase the mobility of pieces.

5. **Threat Avoidance**: Avoid moves that immediately put the player in check.

6. **Tactical Awareness**: Identify and respond to discovered attacks, pins, and skewers.

7. **Pawn Structure**: Promote pawns when possible, especially toward the endgame.

For move selection:

2. Then calculate a weighted score for each move based on:
   - Material gain/loss
   - King safety impact
