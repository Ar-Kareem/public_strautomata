The strategy for this policy is a weighted material-and-position evaluation combined with a shallow search. Since we cannot use an external engine like Stockfish, the policy calculates a "static score" for each possible board state resulting from a move.

1.  **Material Valuation**: Standard values (P=1, N/B=3, R=5, Q=9).
2.  **Positioning**: 
    - **Central Control**: Bonus for pieces occupying or attacking the center (d4, d5, e4, e5).
    - **Development**: Small penalties for pieces remaining on their back rank and bonuses for castling.
    - **King Safety**: Penalties for keeping the King in the center (unless it's the endgame).
3.  **One-Move Lookahead (Quiescence-lite)**: For every legal move, the bot simulates the resulting board, calculates the opponent's best immediate response (based on captures and material), and chooses the move that minimizes the opponent's best possible score (minimax logic).
4.  **Special Actions**: Prioritizes moves that result in Checkmate, then Promotions (Queen), then Captures of high-value pieces.
