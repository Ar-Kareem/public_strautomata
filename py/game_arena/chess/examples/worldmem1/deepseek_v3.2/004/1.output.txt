**Strategy:**

This policy uses a combination of material evaluation, positional considerations, and a minimax search with alpha-beta pruning to depth 3. Key features:

1. **Material Evaluation**: Standard piece values (pawn=1, knight/bishop=3, rook=5, queen=9) with slight bonuses for bishops in open positions and rooks on open files.

2. **Positional Scoring**: 
   - Piece-square tables for each piece type encourage centralization and good positioning.
   - Pawn structure evaluation (doubled/isolated pawn penalties).
   - King safety: penalize moving pawns in front of castled king.
   - Mobility: bonus for number of legal moves available.

3. **Search Algorithm**: Minimax with alpha-beta pruning to depth 3, with quiescence search to avoid horizon effects. Includes move ordering (captures first) and transposition table for efficiency.

4. **Special Prioritization**:
   - Immediate checkmates are selected instantly.
   - Promotions to queen are highly valued.
   - Castling is encouraged in early/mid-game.
   - Captures of higher-value pieces are prioritized.

5. **Opening Preference**: Uses a small opening book for common openings (e4, d4 responses) to avoid early blunders.

The evaluation is balanced between material and positional factors, and the search ensures reasonable tactical awareness within the 1-second time constraint.
