4. **King Safety:** Consider moves that improve the king's safety, such as castling or moving the king away from the center.
5. **Piece Development:** Develop pieces to active squares. Prioritize developing knights and bishops early in the game.
6. **Pawn Structure:** Advance pawns to control the center of the board. Avoid weakening the pawn structure.
7. **Move Ordering:** Checkmates > captures > checks > king safety > piece development > pawn structure.
