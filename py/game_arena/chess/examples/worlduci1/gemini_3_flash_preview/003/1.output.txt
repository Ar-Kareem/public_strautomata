The strategy for this policy is to implement a lightweight chess engine using a Simplified Evaluation Function (PeSTO-style tables) combined with a shallow Minimax search. 

Key features:
1.  **Material Weighting**: Standard values (P=100, N=320, B=330, R=500, Q=900, K=20000).
2.  **Piece-Square Tables (PST)**: Encourages development (knights to center, pawns taking space, kings seeking safety).
3.  **Legal Move Generation**: Since the environment provides legal moves elsewhere but expects a complete script here, I include a minimalist move generator (pseudo-legal check and legality filter) to evaluate future states.
4.  **One-Move Lookahead (Quiescence-lite)**: It prioritizes captures and immediate material gains, while also preventing immediate hanging pieces.
