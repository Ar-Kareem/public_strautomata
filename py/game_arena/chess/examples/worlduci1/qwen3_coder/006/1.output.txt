The strategy for this policy is to implement a simple but effective chess engine that evaluates the board position and selects a strong move. The policy will:

1. Parse the board state and identify piece positions
2. Evaluate the position based on material balance and king safety
3. For each legal move, simulate the move and evaluate the resulting position
4. Select the move that leads to the most favorable position

Key features of the policy:
- Material counting with standard piece values
- King safety consideration through distance from center
- Look-ahead simulation for immediate tactics
- Proper handling of promotions and castling

The policy will prioritize checkmate, captures, and improvements in position evaluation.

PIECE_VALUES = {
    'P': 100,
    'N': 320,
    'B': 330,
    'R': 500,
    'Q': 900,
    'K': 20000  # King value is high to prioritize safety
}

            best_move = move

    if best_move:
        return best_move
        
    # Otherwise return the first legal move
    return legal_moves[0]
