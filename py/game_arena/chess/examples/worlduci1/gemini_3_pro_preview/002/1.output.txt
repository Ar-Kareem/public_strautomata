The strategy for this policy is to implement a robust Chess engine within the strict single-file constraints. It uses a 1D list board representation and implements a custom move generator. The generator produces strictly legal moves by filtering out any moves that leave the king in check. Due to the stateless nature of the input (no history for En Passant or Castling rights), the generator conservatively excludes Castling and En Passant moves to prevent illegal outputs, unless `legal_moves` are explicitly provided by the environment (detected via variadic arguments). The decision-making uses a Depth-2 Minimax search with Alpha-Beta pruning, evaluating positions based on Material difference and Piece-Square Tables (PST) to guide piece development and center control.
