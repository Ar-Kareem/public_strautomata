**Game Analysis:**
- In 4x4 Tic Tac Toe, the winning condition is typically 4 in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).
- This is a relatively simple game where the first player can force a win or at least not lose.
- A strong policy should:
  1. Check for immediate winning moves (complete a line of 4)
  2. Block opponent's immediate winning moves
  3. Take the center if available (positions (1,1), (1,2), (2,1), (2,2))
  4. Take corners if available
  5. Otherwise, take any available position

**Strategy:**
Since 4x4 is small enough, I can use a simple heuristic approach:
1. First, check all lines (rows, columns, diagonals) to see if I can win immediately
2. Then check if opponent can win immediately and block them
3. Prioritize center positions (best for controlling the board)
4. Prioritize corners
5. Take any remaining empty cell
