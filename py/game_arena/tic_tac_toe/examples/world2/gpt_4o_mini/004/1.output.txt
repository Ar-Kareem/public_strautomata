The policy will evaluate the board to identify potential winning moves for the AI, as well as blocked moves for the opponent. The AI will prioritize moves that lead to an immediate victory, followed by moves that block the opponent from winning. If no immediate wins or blocks are available, the AI will choose an optimal position to further its chances for future turns, focusing on the center and corners.

The steps in the policy are as follows:
1. Check for immediate winning moves for the AI and execute them if available.
2. Check for immediate blocking moves against the opponent and execute them if available.
3. Choose the optimal cell to place the move based on maximizing future potential.
