1. **Winning Move Detection**: First, check for any immediate winning move (two own marks and one empty in a row/column/diagonal) and play it.
2. **Blocking Opponent Wins**: If no winning move exists, simulate potential opponent wins by checking lines with no own marks but at least two uncertain cells. This assumes the opponent has placed two marks in such a line and is one move away from winning. Play in the first such uncertain cell to block.
3. **Safe Move Selection**: If no immediate threats are detected, prioritize safe moves with high strategic value: center, corners, then edges. This avoids unblocked opponent wins and leverages strong positions.
