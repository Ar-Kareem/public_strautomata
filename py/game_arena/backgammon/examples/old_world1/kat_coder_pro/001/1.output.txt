The logic for this policy is to implement a greedy approach that prioritizes safety and maximizing progress towards bearing off. The policy will:

1. First handle any checkers on the bar by trying to re-enter them safely
2. Then try to make advances that either:
   - Create or maintain safe points (1 checker)
   - Move checkers as far forward as possible
   - Avoid leaving blots (single checkers) when possible
3. Use the higher die first when possible to maximize progress
4. When bearing off is possible, prioritize:
   - Bearing off checkers from the highest points first
   - Moving checkers into the home board when not all checkers are home

The policy will evaluate all legal moves and select the one that maximizes a score based on these priorities.
