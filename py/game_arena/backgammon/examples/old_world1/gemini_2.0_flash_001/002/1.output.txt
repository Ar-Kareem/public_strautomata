Let's outline a backgammon strategy and then implement it in the `policy` function. The strategy will prioritize:

1.  **Moving off the bar:** If there are checkers on the bar, the first priority is to move them back into the game.
2.  **Hitting opponent checkers:** If possible, hit opponent checkers to put them on the bar and disrupt their game.
3.  **Making points:** Create blocks of two or more checkers on a point to make it harder for the opponent to move past.
4.  **Moving towards home board:** Move checkers towards the home board to prepare for bearing off.
5.  **Bearing off:** If all checkers are in the home board, bear them off as quickly as possible.
6.  **Avoiding leaving blots:** Avoid leaving single checkers (blots) that can be easily hit by the opponent.
7.  **Playing the highest die:** If only one die can be played, play the higher die. If both can be played, try to play both.
