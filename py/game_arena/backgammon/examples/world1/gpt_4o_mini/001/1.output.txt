The strategy for this policy is to prioritize bearing off when possible while ensuring safety and maximization of the board position. The following approach is taken:

1. **Check if on the bar**: If there are any checkers on the bar (`my_bar > 0`), the policy will move those checkers first.
  
2. **Bearing off**: If all checkers are in the home board and there are checkers available for bearing off, then prioritize bearing off.

3. **Maximize moves**: If both dice can be played and valid moves can be made, always utilize higher-value moves first.

4. **Block and avoid opponent**: If not bearing off, look for safe moves that do not land on points occupied by two or more opponent checkers.

5. **Use both dice**: Make moves corresponding to both die rolls if possible, choosing the higher value die first for the move.
