The strategy for this policy is a greedy heuristic that prioritizes re-entering checkers from the bar (highest priority), bearing off checkers when possible (very high priority), and then minimizing the distance of remaining checkers to the home board (points 0-5). For each die (higher and lower), it selects the eligible starting point that maximizes an assigned priority score based on the anticipated new position after the move. If no legal moves are possible for a die, it uses 'P'. The order ('H' or 'L') is determined by whether the original first die is the higher or lower value. This approach is simple, fast, and effective against basic opponents by focusing on safety and progress while respecting legal constraints enforced by the engine.
