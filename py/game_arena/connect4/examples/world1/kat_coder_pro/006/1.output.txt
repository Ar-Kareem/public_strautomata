The strategy for this policy is to use a Minimax algorithm with alpha-beta pruning and a depth-limited search. The policy evaluates the board state using a heuristic that rewards potential winning configurations and penalizes opponent threats. It searches for the best move by exploring possible future game states up to a certain depth, choosing the move that maximizes the score while assuming the opponent will minimize it. The heuristic function assigns scores based on the number of consecutive discs in potential winning lines, giving higher weight to completed lines and near-completions.
