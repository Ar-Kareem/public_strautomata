The goal is to prioritize immediate winning moves, then block the opponent's potential wins, and finally choose moves that lead to longer-term strategies for winning.

Here's the approach breakdown:
1. **Immediate Win Check**: If I can win on the next move, I will choose that column.
2. **Block Opponent**: If the opponent is one move away from winning, I will block that column.
3. **Prioritize Center Column**: The center column gives the greatest potential for connecting four.
4. **Minimax with Depth Limiting**: For further strategy, a simplified version of the Minimax algorithm will evaluate the potential future moves, ensuring that the chosen move maximizes my advantage while minimizing the opponent's options.
