The strategy for this policy is to implement a hybrid approach combining threat detection, immediate win blocking, and proactive path building. For each turn:

1. First, check if we can win immediately by placing a stone that completes a connection between our two sides. If yes, play that move.
2. Second, check if the opponent can win on their next turn. If yes, block that threat.
3. Third, use a modified A* algorithm to find the shortest path across the board for our color, prioritizing moves that:
   - Connect to existing stones
   - Are near the edge (for our target sides)
   - Have high connectivity potential (degree of adjacency to our stones)
4. For tie-breaking, prefer moves closer to the center to maximize flexibility.
5. For the edge cases (first moves), play near the center (5,5) to maximize potential expansion in all directions.
