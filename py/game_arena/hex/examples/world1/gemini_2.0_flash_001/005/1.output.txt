The strategy for this policy is a combination of heuristics:

1. **Bridge Completion:** Prioritize moves that complete immediate bridges (two stones one empty cell apart) to strengthen connections.
2. **Edge Connection:** For black, prioritize moves close to the top and bottom edges to facilitate connection. For white, prioritize moves close to the left and right edges.
3. **Center Control:** In the early game, attempt to occupy central positions, as these provide the most potential connections.
4. **Threat Defense:** Identify and block the opponent's immediate threats (moves that would create a bridge or extend a connection).
5. **Random Legal Move:** If no other heuristic applies, choose a random legal move to avoid illegal actions. This ensures the policy always returns a valid move.
6. **Connectivity heuristic**: Evaluate the connectivity of a candidate move to existing stones of the same color. This can be implemented to improve the strength of the play.
