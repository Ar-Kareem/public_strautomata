2. If the hand value is less than the target score, and there's still a chance of reaching very close to it without going over, the strategy will lean towards hitting.
3. If the hand value is equal to or really close to the target score (but under it), the strategy will stay to avoid the risk of busting.
4. If the hand value exceeds the target score or if it is significantly close to the target (above the target), the strategy will stay.
